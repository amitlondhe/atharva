Atharva Ayurveda Chikitsalaya & Panchakarma Kendra, Thane

ATHARVA - Passage to Quality Life Style

Ayurved is not only a pathy to deal with diseases and illness but it is a complete lifestyle which helps the mankind to live a healthy life physically, psychologically, emotionally, spiritually and socially. It is a holistic approach towards life. The Ayurved principles are enlighted by our Hrishi Munis and our own ancient science; with the help of Ayurvedic medicines, Panchakarma Procedures and Yogashastra.

Welcome to Atharva Ayurved Chikitsalaya and Panchakarma Kendra. This Kendra works on the same principles of Ayurveda and is a healing home for patients of all types of acute & chronic diseases. We are very proud to inform you that through our two centers, we are in the service of thousands of people since last fourteen years.

The main objective of this Kendra is to help the people to live healthy and quality lifestyle by educating them about Ayurveda while treating their acute and chronic diseases.

Further this Kendra also render its services in Health Check up Programs, Medical Camps for poor and needy people of our society in collaboration with other organizations & trusts.
